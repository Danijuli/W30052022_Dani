Introduction to AI

In this course, you will learn the basics of artificial intelligence, including machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. You will also learn about the various applications of AI and the impact it is having on different industries. Our AI assistant will guide you through the course and provide you with personalized feedback and support.

Students will work on projects to apply the knowledge they gain in this course. They can learn at home, by having live virtual classes or accessing pre-recorded video classes.
